About

I am a Joint PhD researcher at BITS Pilani Hyderabad and La Trobe University, focusing on adaptive scheduling and resource allocation in 6TiSCH-based IoT networks for smart city applications. My work aims to enable scalable, energy-efficient, and reliable communication under dynamic and bursty network conditions.

I am supervised by Prof. Nikumani Choudhury (BITS Pilani Hyderabad) and Prof. Wei Xiang (La Trobe University), and contribute to the Asian Smart Cities Research and Innovation Network (ASCRIN) research network.

Research Contributions

ASCP (IEEE ICC 2026)

An analytical model and control policy for slotframe adaptation, enabling predictable latency and efficient resource utilization in 6TiSCH networks.

FlexiFrame (IEEE GLOBECOM 2025)

An adaptive slotframe scheduling mechanism that dynamically adjusts slotframe size based on traffic load to improve energy efficiency and latency in 6TiSCH networks.

TARA (IEEE ANTS 2025)

A topology-aware scheduling approach that allocates resources based on node position and traffic load to enhance reliability and load balancing in multi-hop IoT networks.
